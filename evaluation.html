The page shows prompts that you can experiment with using any LLM of your choice.

Activity 1: Accounting software

You are an HCI expert. I am looking into evaluating an accounting software and I need help in making decisions about the following points: 1-What do you think are the top 3 aspects to evaluate? 2-Can it be evaluated in a lab setting? 3-Will it benefit from an experimental method for evaluation? To evaluate what? 4-If you must choose between UX-expert evaluation or users (accountants) evaluation, which will you use?
